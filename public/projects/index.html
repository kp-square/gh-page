Quality of Life Analysis in San Antonio

January 2, 2025 This project represents a collaborative effort between the Better Future Institute (BFI) of San Antonio and Clemson University. The primary dataset consisted of 311 non-emergency service requests collected over a 14-month period, which was provided to the research team. This dataset was subsequently enriched with demographic and socio-economic data for San Antonio, sourced from the U.S. Census Bureau, to facilitate a comprehensive analysis.

Gaussian Shading With EDICT

December 4, 2024 This research implements Exact Diffusion Inversion via Coupled Transformation (EDICT) with the Gaussian Shading watermarking technique in diffusion models. The results show a slight improvement in the performance of Gaussian Shading.
